Direct Repair Program

Direct repair programs offer policyholders fast claims and repair service after an accident. In fact,
when you choose a direct repair program, you don't have to get damage estimates or schedule an
appointment with an adjuster. All you do after an accident is call your insurance agent or claims
office, choose a pre-approved repair shop and take your car directly there.

Peters Body Shop has been pre-approved for direct repair by most of the major insurance companies.
Choosing Peters Body Shop with the direct repair option saves you time and you still get the high
level of service you expect. Direct repair holds the potential to reduce the time from accident to
repair by three or four days. That gets you back on the road faster.

Here's how it works:

After being involved in an accident, call your insurance agent or local claims office. Choose Peters
Body Shop on the direct repair list, and your claim will be sent electronically to our shop. We then
send an estimate back to the claims office and, with your approval, we begin repairs -- sometimes
even the same day. When your vehicle is repaired, you pay any applicable deductibles from your auto
policy directly to our shop, the remainder of the bill is sent to your insurance provider for payment.
That's it; you're done.

Insurance companies carefully select repair shops based on their quality of work, timeliness of service and previous experience with claims offices. A shop's size and whether it can do most of the repairs in-house also figure in to the equation. Some situations, such as when a car is totaled or when it has been stolen and then recovered, do not qualify for direct repair.

Peters Body Shop Limited Warranty

We have confidence in our work and back it up with a limited warranty for as long as you own your
car. The conditions of the warranty are as follows:

All warranty coverage is for labor only. Rust repairs are excluded.

Metalwork: A lifetime warranty is provided for all metalwork, to include welding and the application
of materials utilized in making collision repairs against cracking, flaking, pitting or deterioration.
We will repair and repaint any metalwork warranted.

Painting, Stripes and Decals: A two year warranty is provided for all work related involving priming
and painting against solvent blistering, peeling, hazing, and excessive loss of pigmentation, and a
one year warranty on the application and adhesion of decorative stripes and decals, excluding those
defects which are caused by extreme environmental conditions to which the vehicle may be exposed.
We will re-prime, repaint, re-stripe or re-decal the specific section or sections of the vehicle
warranted.

Mechanical Repairs: A one year warranty is provided for mechanical repairs pertaining directly to
original collision damage.

Parts: A five year warranty is provided for parts unless the manufacturer’s warranty is less or more, on new parts install by us. Our warranty does not exceed the manufacturer’s warranty.
